Section Notes

01 — Top Bar
Announcement + Contact + Social
Lead with "50–75% Off Contract-Brand Furniture" — positions savings against new dealers immediately. Phone + Request Info for direct B2B contact. Social icons right-aligned, thin line style.
Strategy Copy
02 — Navigation
Sticky nav, charcoal bg, real logo
Real FLIP logo on dark charcoal. Nav items: Furniture · Services · Office Spaces · Portfolio · About · Contact. Orange "Get a Project Quote" CTA — primary conversion action. Stays sticky on scroll.
UX
03 — Hero
Split layout: headline left, photography right
Left: charcoal bg. Headline = "Contract-quality furnishings. Pre-owned intelligence." Orange rule divider. Subhead calls out HM/Steelcase/Knoll by name. Two CTAs: Browse Inventory (orange) + Request Quote (outline). Right: real office installation photography — show a finished, design-forward space, not a product shot. Bottom badge overlays brand names.
Strategy Copy UX
04 — Stats Strip
4 proof stats on orange band
Left-to-right priority: 50–75% savings · 100M lbs diverted · 40K sq ft · 30+ years. Savings leads — it's the primary differentiator vs new dealers. Environmental stat second — Davies positions this prominently too.
Strategy
05 — Three Pillars
Quality · Savings · Sustainability
NEW SECTION — doesn't exist on current site. This is the strategic core of the rebrand. Each pillar needs a killer one-liner + 2–3 proof sentences. Bottom tag lines act as scannable proof points. Hover state: orange tint to signal interactivity.
Strategy Copy
06 — Featured Products
4-col product grid, brand names prominent
Product names should lead with brand (e.g. "Herman Miller Aeron" not just "Task Chair"). Hot Deal + Low Stock badges create urgency. "Add to Project Quote" CTA — keeps B2B framing. Section subhead: "Premium pre-owned inventory from the brands you'd specify new."
Copy UX
07 — Why FLIP
Split: service pillars left, brands right
Left (white): Full-service delivery · Account management · Inventory depth. Right (charcoal): Brand tiles — HM, Steelcase, Knoll, Haworth, Fritz Hansen, Kimball. Key copy: "These aren't substitutes — they're the same products, acquired smarter." CTA: Browse All Inventory.
Strategy Copy
08 — Services
6-card grid, numbered
01 Space Planning · 02 Delivery & Install · 03 Moving/Reconfig · 04 Liquidation · 05 Asset Mgmt · 06 Disaster Recovery. Hover: orange tint. This section builds confidence that FLIP is a full-service partner, not just a reseller.
UX
09 — Industries
6-card grid with left-border hover accent
Medical · Government · Education · Professional Services · Logistics · Stockton Local. Each card should speak to compliance/budget realities of that sector. Orange left-border on hover signals the link destination. "Learn more" arrow links to dedicated industry landing pages.
Strategy UX
10 — Sustainability
Split: big stat left (orange), copy right (charcoal)
100M lbs and 90%+ reuse rate as dominant visual numbers. Right side: position this as a procurement advantage, not just a feel-good stat. "Full chain-of-custody documentation" is the killer line for gov/healthcare buyers. Mirror Davies' "LEED credits" angle — FLIP's equivalent is the documented outcome report.
Strategy Copy
11 — Liquidation CTA Band
Two-col: copy left, CTA right
"Outgrowing your current furniture?" — hooks the other side of the relationship (sell/liquidate, not just buy). Creates a full circular economy story. CTA: "Schedule a Free Assessment." Orange top border signals importance without a full-color section.
Strategy
12 — Newsletter
Charcoal band, email + subscribe
Reframe from generic "get updates" to: "Large lots from Herman Miller and Steelcase arrive frequently. Procurement managers subscribe for early access." This makes the list feel like an insider advantage, not a marketing list.
Copy
13 — Footer
4-col: brand + 3 nav columns
Logo inverted white. Tagline: "Northern California's pre-owned office furniture specialist for over 30 years." Slogan: "Same brands. Smarter buy. Nothing wasted." Columns: Furniture · Services · Company. Contact info in brand column.
Copy
