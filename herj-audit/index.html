03Detailed Findings
#1 Paid Search (Google Ads) 0/85

No active Google Ads campaigns detected for either domain. In healthcare/lab services, competitors are actively bidding on high-intent keywords like "COVID testing near me", "lab tests [city]", and "drug testing services."

#2 AI/Answer Engine Optimization 10/80

No structured data (JSON-LD) for FAQ, HowTo, or MedicalBusiness. Not appearing in AI Overviews for relevant healthcare queries. No FAQ sections on service pages. Zero AEO readiness in a space where AI search is rapidly growing.

#3 Technical SEO 15/90

Conflicting SEO plugins detected — Yoast referenced in robots.txt while Rank Math is generating sitemaps. This causes unpredictable indexing behavior. Sitemap structure is fragmented. Robots.txt directives may be blocking important pages.

#4 On-Page SEO 20/88

Both sites missing H1 tags on homepages — a critical on-page signal. Thin or missing meta descriptions across service pages. No internal linking strategy. Duplicate content risk between the two domains for overlapping services.

#5 Backlink Profile 20/75

Low Domain Rating for both sites. Minimal referring domains. No evidence of link building activity. Healthcare directories and local citations are underutilized. Competitor backlink profiles are significantly stronger.

#6 Core Web Vitals 25/90

mycarelabs.com has real-user (CrUX) data and is FAILING. LCP exceeds 4s, CLS above 0.25. mycareintl.com has no CrUX data (insufficient real-world traffic to measure). Lighthouse lab scores are also poor for both.

#7 E-E-A-T Signals 25/85

Healthcare sites require strong E-E-A-T. Missing author bios, no medical credentials displayed, thin "About" pages. No schema markup for MedicalBusiness or Physician. Google's quality raters would flag these gaps.

#8 Local SEO 40/85

GBP listing exists but is not fully optimized — missing service categories, incomplete business description, limited photos. NAP (Name, Address, Phone) inconsistencies detected across directories. No local landing pages for service areas.

#9 User Experience 45/88

Outdated design elements on both sites. Mobile usability issues detected. Page load times exceed 4s on key landing pages. No clear conversion paths or CTAs above the fold.

#10 Reputation Management 45/85

Google Business Profile exists with decent review volume. However, no active review response strategy — many reviews go unanswered. No review generation system in place. Reputation score could improve significantly with basic management.

04Priority Action Roadmap
P0 Fix conflicting SEO plugins

Choose either Yoast or Rank Math — not both. Conflicting directives are causing unpredictable indexing. This is actively harming both sites.

P0 Add H1 tags to all homepages

Both mycarelabs.com and mycareintl.com are missing the primary heading tag. This is one of the most fundamental on-page SEO signals.

P0 Implement Core Web Vitals fixes

mycarelabs.com is FAILING real-user metrics. Focus on LCP optimization (image compression, server response time) and CLS fixes (explicit image dimensions).

P1 Add structured data (JSON-LD)

Implement MedicalBusiness, LocalBusiness, and FAQ schema across both sites. Critical for healthcare E-E-A-T and AI Overview eligibility.

P1 Build E-E-A-T content

Add physician/staff bios with credentials, certifications, and photos. Expand "About" pages. Add trust signals (accreditations, lab certifications).

P1 Launch Google Ads for high-intent keywords

Competitors are actively capturing paid search traffic. Start with branded terms and high-intent service keywords (lab testing, COVID testing, drug testing + city).

P1 Optimize Google Business Profiles

Complete all GBP fields, add service categories, upload photos regularly, respond to all reviews within 24 hours.

P2 Create local landing pages

Build city-specific service pages to capture local search demand. Target each service area with unique content.

P2 Develop internal linking strategy

Connect service pages, location pages, and blog content with deliberate internal links to distribute authority.

P2 Implement review generation system

Automate review requests post-visit. Respond to all existing reviews. Target 4.5+ star average.
